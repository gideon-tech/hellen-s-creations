Our Story

Hellen Creations was founded to address the gap between talented women artisans in Uganda and the global market. We saw incredible skill and creativity going unrecognized — and we set out to change that.

Our artisans create handcrafted crochet bags, beaded bags, prize bags, seedbead jewelry (necklaces, earrings, waist beads, and anklets), and carpets (after-drags) — each piece a testament to their skill and dedication.

What makes us different: we showcase the women making the crafts on our website. This proves to buyers that the craftspeople are the real makers — not a faceless middleman.

Each product sold contributes a fee back to Hellen Creations to fund further training, separate from the crafters' own pricing and shipment costs. Every purchase is an investment in a woman's future.
