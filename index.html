Our Mission & Story

Hellen Creations empowers women artisans across Uganda by providing hands-on skills training in crochet, beadwork, and carpet weaving. Every product sold directly supports the craftswoman who made it.

We believe in transparency — our website showcases the women making the crafts, proving to buyers that the craftspeople are the makers. Each bag sold contributes a fee back to Hellen Creations to fund further training, separate from the crafters' pricing and shipment costs.
