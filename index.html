Our Story

Our Mission

To empower women artisans across Uganda by providing hands-on skills training and connecting their handmade products to global buyers.

Our Vision

A world where every Ugandan woman artisan has the skills, tools, and market access to sustain herself and her community through her craft.

Our Goal

Train 500 women in marketable craft skills and establish sustainable market connections that directly support artisan livelihoods.

About Hellen Creations

Hellen Creations is a Uganda-based social enterprise and NGO dedicated to empowering women through artisan skills training and connecting their handmade products to global markets.

Our artisans create handcrafted crochet bags, beaded bags, seedbead jewelry, and carpets — each piece a testament to their skill. We showcase the women making the crafts, proving to buyers that the craftspeople are the real makers.

Each product sold contributes a fee back to Hellen Creations to fund further training, separate from the crafters' own pricing and shipment costs.
